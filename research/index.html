Quantum-Inspired Local Tensor Regression (QILTR)

Shetty Nihal Naveen, Ashil Shetty, B Karthik Udupa

Sahyadri College of Engineering and Management, Mangaluru · 2026

Recasts local tensor regression as a Riemannian optimization problem on the manifold of quantum states. Bures distance replaces Euclidean kernels for better-conditioned local Hessians. 4.9× improvement in outlier robustness, +1.22% on QM9 benchmark.
